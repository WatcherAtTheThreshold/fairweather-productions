About Our Sacred Prints & What You Receive

🖨️ Archival Giclée Printing
All prints are professionally produced using archival-quality giclée printing on museum-grade paper. This method ensures rich, accurate color reproduction and lasting durability.

🧵 Premium Fine Art Paper
Most offerings are printed on Somerset Velvet—a soft-textured, heavyweight cotton paper known for its exceptional surface feel and image clarity.

🖼️ Matting & Presentation
Each 8×10" print is matted to a standard 11×14" size using acid-free archival mats, ready for easy framing. Every package includes a protective sleeve and is wrapped with care and intention.

✍️ Artist Touches & Sacred Extras
Each piece is hand-signed and may include a sacred story card, blessing, or handwritten note—depending on the item. These aren't just prints. They're sacred vessels—infused with meaning, prepared with care, and offered with love.
